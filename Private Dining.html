Private Dining Experience

Celebrate Intimately, Dine Luxuriously

Welcome to our exclusive private dining experience at Annapurna Nakashtra Restaurant. Whether you're hosting a birthday celebration, an anniversary, a business dinner, or a family gathering, our private dining rooms provide the perfect blend of elegance, privacy, and personalized service.

Enjoy a curated menu by our executive chef, tailored ambiance, and attentive service to make your occasion truly special. We offer both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, and our team is happy to accommodate dietary preferences.

Available for small groups (8–20 guests) or larger functions with advance notice. Each private space is beautifully decorated and equipped for an unforgettable culinary journey.
